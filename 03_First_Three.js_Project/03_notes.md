# 4 basic elements for three.js

- Scene
- Object (is called Mesh): 
  - it is the combination of Geometry (shape) and Material (how it looks)
- Camera: 
  - It is the theorical point of view used when rendering
  - It needs the **Field of View** and the **Aspect Ratio** (width / height)
- Renderer
  - It will render the scene seen from the camera's point of view
